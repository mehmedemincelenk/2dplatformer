# 🎮 False Knight

**Theme:** Fracture (Kırılma)

## 📖 The Story
A middle school student who dreams of becoming a knight is tragically thrown off a rooftop by bullies. Falling into a deep coma, he finds himself in a dreamworld where he manifests as a noble knight, fighting against monsters that are manifestations of his real-life bullies. Throughout the journey, he experiences "fractures" where the line between the dreamworld and reality begins to blur.

In the final battle, a "Big Boss" with randomized behavior patterns defeats the knight—not to end his journey, but to shatter the dream and wake him from his coma.

## 🧠 Theme Interpretation
The "Fracture" theme is explored through the character's psychological state—the shifting boundary between the physical world and the subconscious mind. This is visually and emotionally represented through "cracks" in the game world and the narrative conflict between a child's dream and a harsh reality.
